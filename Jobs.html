As a new company, Ceramic Pottery is searching for new employees to help release its art.

Job List

  1. Cashier: Takes money from customers, give them change, and the products(s) they purchase
  2. Stocker: Organize pottery by putting the same pottery together
  3. Pottery Maker: This guy or girl takes clay and literally molds it into a piece of artwork
  4. Pottery Decorator: This person decorates the pottery after it's put into the kiln for the first time
  5. Pottery Baker: When the pottery maker finishes building his pottery and when the painter finishes
    painting the pottery, the pottery baker puts the pottery in the kiln and bakes it in the kiln
  6. Supervisor: You can be supervisor of employees that all have any one type of job.
    Though you must be an expert in that field as well as have taken several
    college courses in business.
