Introducing Myself

Hello. My name is Lawrence Rosin. I am the 30 year old founder and
current owner of Ceramic Pottery.

Become Owner of Ceramic Pottery

I started taking art courses in high school as requirements. Though it was
required, I eventually realized I loved art. From there I started taking several art
classes. After graduating I was unsure whether I should be a painter, make
pottery, or animate. So I majored in art, and taken many classes in all three of the areas I
considered working at. Eventually, I loved how the clay felt, so I decided to get into
ceramics. After some searching, I found a job at the Art Museum as a pottery maker. After
5 years, I started saving money for my future ceramic store. And 5 years after that, I had
enough money to open it. I though since it was pottery which is also ceramics, I decided to
call it Ceramic Pottery.
